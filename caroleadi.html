Meet Carole

Carole is a highly experienced, fully qualified driving instructor, serving Bromley, Croydon, and surrounding areas with a commitment to helping students succeed. Renowned for her high pass rate, Carole’s friendly and calm teaching style ensures that each lesson is both enjoyable and impactful. She believes that fun, engaging sessions lead to quicker learning, fewer lessons, and better retention, empowering students to become confident, safe drivers for life. Growing up in Bromley, Carole has an in-depth knowledge of local routes, providing invaluable insights into area-specific driving challenges. Her extensive background working with young people highlights her patience and adaptability, allowing her to tailor each lesson to the individual needs of her students. With a genuine passion for teaching and a warm, supportive approach, Carole fosters a comfortable learning environment, making each lesson as effective as it is enjoyable.

  • Experienced and Patient: With years of experience, Carole teaches at a pace that suits each learner.
  • Local Knowledge: Born locally, Carole knows routes in Bromley, and Croydon very well.
  • Supportive Approach: A calm, encouraging instructor to ensure each lesson builds confidence and skill.
  • Adaptable Teaching: Skilled in working with a variety of learners, including those who are anxious or would prefer a female instructor.

Carole's Car - Ford Fiesta Zetec Turbo

The Fiesta’s manageable size allows for confident maneuvering, making parallel parking, tight turns, and lane changes easier for learners. The car’s intuitive layout and responsive handling create an ideal learning environment, helping new drivers progress confidently and safely.

Beginner Lessons

Beginner driving lessons are perfect for those new to driving. Our experienced St Mary Cray instructors create a calm, supportive environment designed to build your confidence and teach essential skills, step-by-step. From mastering the basics to becoming test-ready, we’ll guide you through every lesson, ensuring you feel prepared, comfortable, and fully in control on the road.

Mock Test

Mock driving tests are designed to help you feel confident and prepared for the real exam. Experience common test routes in St Mary Cray, go through a practice run under real conditions, and tackle any nerves in advance. Our mock tests make the official test feel less daunting, so you’ll know you’re ready when the big day arrives.

Practical Driving Test

Your practical driving test is the big moment, and we’ll make sure you’re confident and know exactly what you need to. Beforehand, we’ll take you out for a focused hour of practice to calm any last-minute nerves. Then, it’s down to you to show you’re ready and capable on the road.

Motorway Lessons

Motorway lessons are perfect for newly licensed drivers. These lessons are designed to help you feel confident on motorways, covering essential skills like merging safely, navigating multiple lanes, and handling high-speed traffic. Motorways are key routes to many destinations, mastering these roads is an important step toward fully independent driving.

Refresher Lessons

Refresher lessons are designed for drivers who hold a license but haven’t been behind the wheel in a while, need to prepare for a driving test retake, or want to build up confidence on the road. These lessons help you regain your skills, practice essential driving techniques, and feel fully at ease in the driver’s seat again.
